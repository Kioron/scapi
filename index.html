What is Unturned?

Unturned is a free-to-play survival game developed by Smartly Dressed Games. It is a sandbox game in which you can play in single-player or multiplayer mode. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world, where players must survive against zombies and other players. Players can build bases, scavenge for supplies, and fight off zombies and other players. The game has a large and active community, with many mods and custom maps available. Unturned is available on Steam for Windows, Mac, and Linux.

What is a Roleplay Server?

A roleplay server is a type of multiplayer server where players take on the roles of characters and interact with each other in a virtual world. Players create characters with unique backstories and personalities, and then act out those characters in the game. Roleplay servers can be based on a wide variety of themes, from fantasy worlds to modern cities. Players can engage in a wide range of activities, from socializing and exploring to fighting and questing. Roleplay servers often have rules and guidelines to ensure that players stay in character and create an immersive and enjoyable experience for everyone.
